About Bhaskarprabha Foundation

Bhaskarprabha Foundation is a Ganit Paathshala based on traditional Bhartiya education system (Gurukul system) focused on Ganit Shashtra. Tejas Kulkarni Guruji is the founder of this Ganit Paathshala.

Bhaskarprabha Foundation is in the premises of Sant Sahawas Ved Paathshala in Hingane, Sinhagad Road in Pune, Maharashtra.

This is the first-ever traditional Indian Paathshala dedicated to the study of Ganit (Mathematics). The aim is to revive the study of ancient Bhartiya Ganit. In addition, Modern mathematics is also taught at the Paathshala.

Salient Features

This is the first paathshala to study Ganit mathematics based on the traditional system. In this system, the student normally stays at the Gurukul for learning. However, currently, we also have local students where the staying facility is not provided, and the setting up of the same is in process.

  • Both boys’ and girls’ study at the paathshala
  • Residential pathshala (residential facility is currently not available for girls)
  • Day paathshala for local students
  • Ancient Bhartiya Ganit, Statistics, Computer Science as well as modern mathematics is taught at the paathshala
  • Exams are conducted at Shree Datta Devsthan Trust, Ahilyanagar (formerly known as Ahmednagar)
  • Sanskrit language is the medium of instruction
